## 2. North star

From `docs/vision-mission-control.md` (fly bridge / Internet of Agentic Work) and `docs/design-mc-constellation-ui.md`:

**Mission Control is the fly bridge of the IoAW**: one glass where the principal sees the constellation (topology), is told who needs them (attention), sees whose hands are working and whose are queued (hands), and reads honest link/node vitals (health) — and can act with **signed, accountable, fail-closed govern verbs** without leaving the glass.

The four fused lanes map 1:1 onto the mockup:
1. **Topology** — the D1–D5 constellation, breathing with real traffic.
2. **Attention** — WHO NEEDS ME: approvals, admissions, escalations; every button backed by a real arbitration channel.
3. **Hands** — WORKING across the principal's own stacks; honest `queued — awaiting hands`; drill to session interior on own stacks only.
4. **Health** — READING THE GLASS: PRESENCE/ACTIVITY/TRAFFIC/HEALTH lanes, sourced from signal, honest `unobserved`.

**The wider surface model (2026-07-07 design drop).** Six layout explorations plus two vision revisions (*Metafactory — Vision v3*, *IoAW v2*; artifacts at `docs/mockups/mc-layouts/`) widen the end-state beyond the topology cockpit to **three top-level views plus a ledger**:

- **TOPOLOGY** — this plan's A–D tracks: the constellation + the cockpit (layouts B *Immersive*, C *Bands*, D *Dual* are structural variants of the same fused glass).
- **PEOPLE** (layout E) — humans at the centre: timezone bands (NZT/CET/CT/ET), *"4/7 awake · swarm never sleeps"*, per-person cards distinguishing the human's presence from their swarm's autopilot state.
- **WORK** (layout F) — the marketplace: **form → build → operate** lanes, gigs (people bidding), capacity lending (*"lends 40k tokens/hr · 3 stacks drawing"*), domains beyond engineering (marketing/sales/finance), shipped products with SLO/on-call.
- **SESSIONS** (layout G) — the attribution ledger: *"every session runs on behalf of an accountable human; attribution rolls up spend; signed_by rolls up responsibility"* — session × assistant × FOR-principal × attribution target × model × tokens × cost, with a TODAY rollup.
- Layout B additionally sketches the **PUBLIC MESH** (`public.*` capability marketplace — "come find me") beyond federated networks, and network kinds this plan's roster model already supports (co-op, isolated-private JV).

Two vision principles graduate into cockpit requirements: **presence is human and circadian; activity is the swarm's and constant — they are never the same signal** (invariant 19), and **on-behalf-of must be verifiable, not asserted** — the signed_by chain makes attribution a fact (invariant 20). §4.E carries these as horizon tracks; they extend the four fused lanes, they do not replace them.

Governing principles (each is a hard invariant in §6): **truth-not-theater** (no control without its backend channel; no fabricated liveness), **altitude with sovereignty** (ADR-0005: session/envelope interiors local-only, peers aggregate-only — now code-enforced), **two postures** (admin/member — the deprecated posture word never renders), **daemon-local signing** (seeds never in worker/browser), **sourced-from-signal** (verdicts verbatim, never re-derived), **dispatch-don't-dictate** (glass orchestrates by capability; competence lives at the edges), **sovereign by default** (admission mints nothing — no credential, key, or capability is created as a side effect of admitting).

One vocabulary caveat on the reference artifact itself: the mockup's `posture` enum uses the deprecated pre-migration posture word, and it shows a member-side **Leave network** button — both violate this plan's invariants (8 and 16). Mockup strings are **not** lift-verbatim; CK-3/CK-7 acceptance carries this explicitly.

## 6. Load-bearing invariants (consolidated)

1. **ADR-0005 no-interiors, code-enforced (#1514 S6):** SESSION/ENVELOPE altitude reachable only on the principal's own stacks; federated peers expose aggregate metadata (presence/capabilities/health/counts) at best; **every** new cockpit/aggregation endpoint — including read-only FLG-1/FLG-3/FLG-4/FLG-11 and CK-4a, and SPX-6 event relays — flows THROUGH the worker-scoped `DashboardSnapshot` guard, never beside it.
2. **Truth-not-theater:** no control ships without its backend channel (Approve/Deny requires the runner arbitration channel; awaiting-hands copy requires the capacity queue); health/traffic render honest `unobserved` when signal is absent — never default-green, never default-red; no fabricated liveness, motion, drill state, or dead deep-links (CK-8 cross-stack degrade).
3. **Every glass mutation replicates the hardened decider pattern:** Host-header allowlist + foreign-Origin rejection (FND-6) + identity gate + **authorization binding to the configured principal allowlist** + daemon-signed via `cortex.ts`-injected decider + CF-Access bind-conditioned JWT off-loopback (fail-closed 503 without `cfAccess.aud`) + typed-confirm. **Typed-confirm is an INTENT control (human deliberateness), never counted as a security control** — it is trivially satisfiable programmatically. The stack seed never reaches worker or browser. Every mutating route on the daemon — including `/api/sessions`, `/requeue`, `/abandon`, and attention lifecycle — sits at identity-gate minimum; there is no unauthenticated mutation tier.
4. **Step-up MFA precedes glass exposure of high-blast verbs** — the high-blast set is **seal/admit-and-seal, rotate-K, revoke, escalation-approve** (retire is never a glass verb); enforcement lives at the **daemon decider seam** (never worker-side); the mechanism is loopback-viable (D-2); absent enrollment ⇒ 403 + CLI fallback, never silent downgrade.
5. **Two admin authorities never conflate:** registry-admin (admit/reject, ADR-0020, per-network) vs hub-admin (seal/authorize/revoke/rotate, ADR-0018 Q5, `REGISTRY_HUB_ADMIN_PUBKEYS`) — posture badges and decider wiring keep them distinct or principals see buttons they cannot successfully sign.
6. **Secret-material discipline:** K and per-member PSKs never transit the MC surface, worker, or browser — **including as ciphertext**: the sealed `--seal-only` artifact flows daemon→registry→daemon with the glass only triggering (D-12); only `kid` + SHA-256 8-hex fingerprint are displayable (`network-secret-lib.ts` print discipline is the ceiling — the surface must not add a leakier path).
7. **Hub-locality guard:** hub-half ops (seal conf write, revoke hub-cut, rotate SIGHUP) valid only when the executing daemon's host IS the hub — or MC reintroduces the 2026-07-03 Authorization-Violation storm from the glass.
8. **Fail-closed posture gating:** every govern affordance reuses `isAdminPosture` / `roster_scope==='complete'`; pending rows and govern buttons never render for non-admin posture; members see the participation footer with lifecycle STATE + CLI guidance, never admin verbs or a Leave button. Posture vocabulary: **admin/member** only — the mockup itself uses the deprecated word, so mockup strings are never lifted verbatim (the carve-out gate is case-blind to camelCase; per-site review on cockpit copy).
9. **Three-valued handoff semantics:** a glass attestation upgrades ONLY `undefined`→treated-done, never a real `false` (Sage #1499) — no naive boolean checkbox.
10. **SOURCED-FROM-SIGNAL, never re-derived:** verdict strings verbatim from `system.transport.*`; the `ObservabilityOrigin` monotonic-foreign guard survives every refactor (a peer's verdict is never local health); parity oracle `signal transport roster` ⇔ MC overlay holds through the health-fold refactor; member-network aggregate health only via the curated two-party export — never synthesized locally. **The sideband proxy stays loopback-enforced** — CK-8 extends its use, never its exposure.
11. **Mutation-safety parity with CLI, plus glass-specific concurrency defenses:** C-1483 canaried/dry-run-by-default discipline gets a glass equivalent (preview + typed-confirm + MFA) before any live-member mutation button wires up; live-member mutations additionally carry **daemon-side single-flight per network + client idempotency keys + preview-hash-bound commits** — the browser adds retry and concurrency paths the one-shot CLI never had, and a double-fired rotate is a leaf outage.
12. **Cred-injection precedes off-host execution, and the proxy is a hardened, audited trust point:** tokens live at the proxy, never in the sandbox; no redirect-following after injection; per-sandbox proxy identity (no confused deputy); response-size/rate budgets; sandbox DNS deny-by-default; per-request proxy audit log + enforced per-task egress intent; CO-7 M3's fail-closed gate stays enforced throughout. Host-allowlist is necessary not sufficient (allowlisted-host exfiltration); redaction covers stdout, the ERROR path, tool-server logs, **and the design accounts for timing/inference side-channels** (SPX-11).
13. **Harness preservation across backends:** any remote backend honors the full `CCSessionOpts` contract behind the same CCSession-shaped event stream; LocalBackend convergence is byte-for-byte.
14. **On-behalf-of, never the principal's keys** (PRINCIPLES.md §3): propose→approve→step-up-MFA, signature-traceable to a human; approval verdicts are **single-use and tool-call-instance-bound** (no replay); executing creds scoped, single-use, expiring. AI never touches production directly.
15. **Extend, don't fork:** endpoint kinds extend the `sessions.endpoint_kind` CHECK; origin is a schema field, not a React-key hack; capacity extends `AdmissionGate` (`src/bus/admission/gate.ts`), never a parallel limiter; cockpit folds the existing four views + F-7 drill-down, never rewrites them.
16. **Heavy local-machine mutations stay CLI-side** (join/provision/make-live/leave/retire) — MC may be viewing remotely and cannot drive member-host nsc/launchd/nats mutations; the glass surfaces guided-join progress and lifecycle state instead (the mockup's member-side Leave **button** is a recorded deviation — state + guidance text only). **Deterministic surface formatting:** the cockpit renders structure off the bus in code, never LLM-generated presentation.
17. **Sovereign by default — admission mints nothing:** admitting/authorizing a member creates no credential, key, or capability as a side effect; the member's identity remains self-sovereign (ADR-0013 Model B); FLG-2's "mints nothing, writes no local fs" is an instance of this rule, not an aside.
18. **Every decider decision emits an append-only audit record** (db row or `system.governance.*` event): verb, resolved identity, network/member target, verdict, timestamp, idempotency key. The enterprise posture (SPX-11) will need this trail retroactively — write it from the first verb, not the last.
19. **Presence ≠ activity (IoAW v2):** presence is *human and circadian* (awake/afk/asleep — from the PPL-1 signal, with its consent posture, never derived from agent traffic); activity is *the swarm's and constant* (session counts, envelope flow). The glass never conflates them: a sleeping human with a busy autopilot renders as exactly that, and no human is shown "active" because their agents are.
20. **Attribution is signed, not inferred:** the SESSIONS ledger renders on-behalf-of from session rows and the signed_by chain — *attribution rolls up spend; signatures roll up responsibility*. Unattributed work lands in an honest `unattributed` bucket, never guessed into a project; FOR-principal comes from session identity, never heuristics.
